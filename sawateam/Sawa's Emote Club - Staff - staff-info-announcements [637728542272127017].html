lunaaaaa 20-Dec-21 05:50 AM
There's been a slight miscommunication with newer staff so I need to clarify something: We don't posted licensed series on MangaDex. We post it on our website only. This was decided a long time ago by senior staff. We do that in order to prevent bots from ripping our scans and posting it on aggregator sites. This is to 1) prevent being slammed with a Cease & Desist from licensers and 2) encourage readers to support the official. Yes, of course that doesn't mean nobody else will work on the series, as seen with VLT and HM. However, this is what we do now for licensed series that we continue translating: they become passion projects for staff who still enjoy and want to work on the series despite licensing, and keeping them only on our website ensures they remain passion projects rather than become a grounds for clout chasing. I'm not accusing anyone of working on projects to chase clout, but please note that our stance is that we only work on licensed projects due to passion and not due to readership. That is our no.1 priority for working on licensed stuff, though depending on decisions by group or upper staff, we also work on licensed webtoons out of obligation (Office Blind Date, JOAVW), though I don't see that happening anymore in the future. I know this is disappointing news for staff who had hoped we would mass release 30+ VLT chapters on MD to smash the competition, but that won't be happening. If you are disappointed by that, feel free to message me to talk about it, and you are also free to remove yourself from working on the affected projects. (edited)
